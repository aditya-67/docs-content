## Notifications

##### Outgoing event alerts sent by Setu to partners.

Currently, there is one event—`BILL_SETTLEMENT_STATUS`, which triggers a notification as explained below.

<hr class="primary" />

#### Bill settlement status

###### Bill settled

A `SETTLEMENT_SUCCESSFUL` notification is sent to the biller after successful settlement from Setu to the biller. This depends on the settlement frequency agreed upon between Setu and the biller.
